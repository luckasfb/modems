eService ID: N/A
Description: 
  [Daily Use][Communication][Protocol] Cannot receive MT call
  [root cause]
  MM clears cell_info in mm_no_service_handler while OOS is ongoing, and receives a sys information from lower layer to notify when UE goes back to original cell. However, the sys information is sent in dedicated mode(PS signaling ongoing) without NAS sys infor (is_sys_info_present==KAL_FALSE). In such case MM do not update current cell_info and the current cell_info has been reset in mm_no_service_handler which leads to LAI changed handling path.
  
  In the end of LU, MT call paging is coming in the same cs signaling connection. MM responds to the same signaling connection and no more answer from NW. After t3240 expiry, MM force release connection. The follow paging(even in new CS signaling connection) response are unanswered by NW.
  
  [solution]
  Although the root cause no response from NW, but the unnecessary CS signaling might increase possibility to hit the network abnormal behavior.
  
  MM do need to reset cell_info in mm_no_service_handler, and add a OOS condition checker in mm_ctrl_reg_attach_req_handler to ensure the MM behavior is unchanged.
  
  [how to verify]
  1) Open a PS signaling connection: FP need to dial-up/ 
  2) Enter shielding room, wait 30+ seconds. No coverage display
  3) Leave the room and regain coverage, do MT call immediately
  Check if MT/MO call can be connected or not.
